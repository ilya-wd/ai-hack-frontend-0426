# Technocrats

We use AI agents to make billing invisible for B2B service companies. We're a two-person team: Aleksi Rantala (CEO) and Jimi Bergskaug (CTO). You can meet us both at the hackathon. We're looking to expand our team soon.

## The Challenge

A field technician fixes a leaking pipe, types "fixed pipe 2h lol" on their phone, and calls it a day. Somewhere downstream, a billing manager cries. This is the type of boring but valuable problem where AI agents shine. And you are going to fix it.

Your job is to build an AI agent that turns messy technician messages into correct, validated work logs and invoice line items. The agent has to figure out the customer, the site, the right rate, what materials were probably used, whether the worker was even certified to do the job, and whether someone already did it last week. The technician should barely notice the agent exists. The billing manager should sleep soundly.
